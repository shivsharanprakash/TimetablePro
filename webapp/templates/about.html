About Timetable Pro

We're revolutionizing educational scheduling with intelligent automation and user-friendly design.

Our Mission

Timetable Pro was founded with a simple goal: to eliminate the tedious, time-consuming process of manual timetable creation. We believe educators should focus on teaching, not scheduling conflicts.

Our platform combines advanced algorithms with an intuitive interface, making complex scheduling accessible to institutions of all sizes.

Our Vision

To become the global standard for educational scheduling solutions.

Our Values

Innovation, reliability, and user-centric design drive everything we do.

Why Choose Timetable Pro?

Time Saving

Reduce timetable creation time from weeks to minutes.

Error Reduction

Eliminate scheduling conflicts and double bookings automatically.

Resource Optimization

Maximize utilization of teachers, classrooms, and facilities.

Flexibility

Adapt to changing requirements with easy modifications.

Features

User authentication and project management
Support for multiple batches and years (SY, TY, BTech)
Automatic conflict detection and resolution
Export timetables to DOCX and XLSX formats
Lab and lecture session management
Break time configuration

How to Use

  1. Sign up for a free account or login if you already have one
  2. Create a new timetable project with your configuration
  3. Fill in the required details: classrooms, labs, subjects, and timings
  4. Generate your timetable automatically
  5. View, edit, or download your timetables anytime
